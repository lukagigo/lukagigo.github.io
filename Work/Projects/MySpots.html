Mobile aplication My Spots

The mobile aplication My Spots is intended for a group of friends who want to share their location to each other and help them with organizing social activities. Within the application, the user determines the standard spots where he/she spends the most time and lets their other friends know if he/she is currently at one of them. It answers those classic annoying questions, such as "Where are you?", "What are you doing?", "Who else is there?"... in the form of a systematic color display.

The app falls into the group of tracing apps, but it is a milder version of them since it doesn’t allow 100% stalking. To keep it user friendly it allows the user to choose who will be able to see their current spot for each of their friends seperately.
